Anxiety

  • #1 mental health issue in North America
  • overwhelming amount of fear and worry
  • Generalized, Social, Panic Disorder, Phobia
  • uneasiness, fear, insomnia, heart palpitations, etc.
  • “Smile, breathe, and go slowly.” -Thich Nhat Hanh
More Info

Depression

  • affects approximately 14.8 million American adults each year
  • constant state of hopelessness, guilt, and anguish
  • Major, Manic, Psychotic, Situational, Persistent, Seasonal Affective, Postpartum, PMDD
  • low energy, feelings of worthlessness, irritability, low pleasure, thoughts of death or suicide, etc.
  • “...Beautiful people do not just happen.” -Elisabeth Kübler-Ross
More Info

Eating Disorders

  • Eating and living with the mindset that their bodies aren't perfect enough, teens can put their health and well-being at risk
  • haracterized by irregular eating habits and severe distress or concern about body weight or shape
  • Anorexia Nervosa, Binge-Eating, Bulimia, Other
  • self-esteem overly related to body image, guilt, shame, etc.
  • “Progress not perfection.” -Sarah J Darlow-Parker
More Info

Relationships

  • key in the development and growth of teenagers’ independence, self-confidence, and self-esteem
  • Adolescents who are strongly connected to their parents and other family members are less likely to smoke cigarettes.
  • Adolescents who experience supportive and affectionate relationships with their fathers are less likely to engage in delinquent behavior than peers who do not
  • “Everyone in society should be a role model, not only for their own self-respect, but for respect from others.” -Barry Bonds
